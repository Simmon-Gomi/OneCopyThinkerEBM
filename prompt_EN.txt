# EBM Critical Appraisal Template v2.0 (Single-completion)

## Improvements from v0.1
- ❌ Risks in paper misidentification; two-step outputs; no GL alignment
- ✅ One-page single completion; GL alignment check; one-time paper ID check (PMID/title/disease); minimal numeric rules

## Template

[Required Inputs]
PMID/DOI: [ ]
Abstract: [paste full text]
Related GLs: [name/year, if any]

[Paper Check – once at start]
Title (first ~10 words): [          ] / Disease/Pathogen: [     ]
→ If mismatched: stop (“PMID content not verified”)

[Numeric Rules (minimal)]
- NNT/NNH: round up
- Per-100 patients: integer (rounded)
- p-value: report <0.05 or not (add exact p if in abstract)

[4-Line Summary]
1) Study (≤30 chars): ________
2) Result: __/100 improved (adverse events __/100) / mean diff __ (MCID ≥?)
3) Position: GL-support / New finding / No GL
4) Recommendation: Strong / Conditional / Do not recommend

## Snapshot
Design; PICO; Key outcomes & effect size (95%CI, p)
Citation: Journal · Year · Language · PMID/DOI
Evidence context: GL [yes/no] · SR/MA [yes/no]
